Our Commitment to Sustainability and the SDGs

Shoo is committed to contributing to the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). These global goals provide a blueprint for a better, more sustainable future. Below, we highlight the 5 SDGs that align most closely with our values and actions.

Sustainable Development Goals We Support

SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

We focus on producing high-quality, long-lasting products and use sustainable materials in our designs. We aim to reduce waste by creating shoes that stand the test of time.

SDG 13: Climate Action

Shoo is committed to reducing our carbon footprint by using renewable energy in production and supporting carbon offset initiatives. We aim to fight climate change through every step of our process.

SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth

Shoo supports economic growth by creating job opportunities and ensuring fair wages and working conditions across our supply chain. We believe that good work is key to sustainable development.

SDG 5: Gender Equality

We promote gender equality by providing equal opportunities to women in all levels of our organization and ensuring gender equality in wages and career advancement.

SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation

Shoo is committed to reducing water consumption in our production process and supporting clean water initiatives in the communities where we operate.
